Ranked-Choice Voting Interface

Our system implements India's first ranked-choice voting mechanism in an EVM. Voters can rank candidates by preference, ensuring true majority support and eliminating the "spoiler effect" common in first-past-the-post systems.

  • Intuitive tap-to-rank interface
  • Real-time visual feedback
  • Accessible for all literacy levels
  • Instant vote encryption and storage
  • Complete audit trail maintained
